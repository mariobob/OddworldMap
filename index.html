Settings

Keeps the Display toggles and Objects filters across visits.
Reopens the map where you left off. A shared link always wins.
Game, level and path buttons list their full in-game names.
Clicking or tapping a screen (anywhere that isn't a followable object) opens a panel listing everything on it.
Reveal every field the game stores for objects (Slig shooting behaviour, patrol timings, and more), not just the notable ones, in tooltips and the screen list.
Stores screens you visit (up to ~150 MB) so your next visit doesn't re-download them. Turning it off removes everything stored.
